    • About Engineerinme

Engineerinme at its beginning was a specially created portal for one of the World's Biggest community,
The Engineers' community.
This app is ment for "Budding Engineers" and the "Engineering Aspirants".
"Budding Engineers" meant the fellows who were either in any Engineering College
or were passed out from Engineering college and still felt affinity towards it.
While Engineering Aspirants meant those who wished to take admission in
some good Engineering college and wish to pursue Engineering as their Career.
